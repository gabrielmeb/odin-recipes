Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a hearty and comforting Italian dish that's perfect for family gatherings and special occasions. Layers of tender lasagna noodles are alternated with a rich and savory filling made from seasoned ground beef, sweet Italian sausage, and a flavorful tomato sauce. Between these layers, creamy ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese come together to create a luscious, cheesy goodness. The dish is baked to perfection in the oven, allowing the flavors to meld and the cheese to bubble and brown. Garnished with fresh basil, this lasagna is a true crowd-pleaser, with its delicious combination of pasta, cheese, and flavorful sauce that's bound to satisfy any appetite.

Ingredients:

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese (2 cups)
  • 1 (24-ounce) jar of your favorite marinara sauce
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cups (240g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 12 oven-ready lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 cup (60g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned, then drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the marinara sauce to the cooked beef, along with the dried oregano. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Simmer for a few minutes.
  4. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella, and 1/4 cup of Parmesan.
  5. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  6. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  7. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  8. Add a layer of meat sauce on top of the cheese mixture.
  9. Repeat the layers, starting with noodles and ending with meat sauce. Finish with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, and the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for about 10 minutes before serving.
