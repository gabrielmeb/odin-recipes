Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Indulge in the creamy comfort of homemade macaroni and cheese. This classic dish is a favorite for both kids and adults. Follow the simple steps below to create a delicious, cheesy macaroni and cheese that will satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (8 ounces) elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups (8 ounces) shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup (2 ounces) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups (720ml) milk
  • 1/4 cup (4 tablespoons) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup (30g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon dry mustard (optional)
  • 1/2 cup (60g) breadcrumbs (for topping, optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the macaroni: In a large pot, boil the macaroni according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the cheese sauce: In the same pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to create a roux. Cook for a couple of minutes until it's lightly browned.
  3. Gradually add the milk to the roux while continuously whisking to avoid lumps. Cook and stir until the mixture thickens, usually about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Reduce heat and add shredded cheddar cheese and grated Parmesan cheese. Stir until the cheese is completely melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Season the cheese sauce with salt, black pepper, paprika, and dry mustard (if using). Adjust the seasonings to your taste.
  6. Combine the cooked macaroni with the cheese sauce and mix thoroughly.
  7. Optional breadcrumb topping: Preheat your oven's broiler. Transfer the macaroni and cheese to an oven-safe dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and broil for a few minutes until the breadcrumbs are golden brown and crispy.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade macaroni and cheese!
